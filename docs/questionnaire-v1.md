# Questionnaire Structure v1.0

Since our  beginner retail investors, every question would answer one of these three objectives:
* Understand who the investor is
* Understand what the investor wants
* Understand whether the portfolio matches those goals

The InvestIQ platform is
- Portfolio intelligence tool
- Risk awareness tool
- Values alignment tool
Therefore, we need less information than traditional investment onboarding.

Updated Questionnaire Structure

Batch 0 — Welcome & Introduction
Purpose
Prepare the user for the experience and increase completion rates.

Content
Let's understand your portfolio better

Short Description
Answer a few quick questions about your investment goals, risk preferences, and portfolio. We'll use your responses to provide personalized insights and help you better understand your investments.

What You'll Get
✓ Portfolio risk analysis
✓ Diversification insights
✓ Goal alignment assessment
✓ Values-based portfolio analysis
✓ Personalized suggestions

Time Estimate
5 short steps • approximately 5–7 minutes

Reassurance Note : No advanced investing knowledge required, we'll guide you through every step.

Batch 3
Risk Profile
Questions 7–9 of 3

Scoring note (internal — not shown to user): Each answer carries a score. Total score maps to a risk profile:
* 3–5 → Conservative
* 6–8 → Moderate
* 9–12 → Aggressive

7. Imagine your portfolio drops 20% in value. What would you most likely do?   Type: Single-select
Answer	Score
Sell everything — I don't want to lose more	1
Sell some and move to safer options	2
Do nothing and wait for it to recover	3
Buy more — it's a good opportunity	4
8. How would you describe your comfort level with investment risk?  Type: Single-select
Answer	Score
Very low — I prefer certainty over growth	1
Low — small fluctuations are okay	2
Medium — I can handle moderate ups and downs	3
High — I'm comfortable with significant swings for higher returns	4
