# Questionnaire Structure V2.0

Since our  beginner retail investors, every question would answer one of these three objectives:
* Understand who the investor is
* Understand what the investor wants
* Understand whether the portfolio matches those goals

The InvestIQ platform is
- Portfolio intelligence tool
- Risk awareness tool
- Values alignment tool
Therefore, we need less information than traditional investment onboarding.

Updated Questionnaire Structure

Batch 0 — Welcome & Introduction
Purpose
Prepare the user for the experience and increase completion rates.

Content
Let's understand your portfolio better

Short Description
Answer a few quick questions about your investment goals, risk preferences, and portfolio. We'll use your responses to provide personalized insights and help you better understand your investments.

Time Estimate
5 short steps • approximately 5–7 minutes

Reassurance Note: No advanced investing knowledge required, we'll guide you through every step.
